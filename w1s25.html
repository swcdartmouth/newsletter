View our Bargaining Tracker!

Columns K & L show the latest proposals in our bargaining sessions with the College. Each article is written independently by our volunteer Organizing Committee—student workers like yourself who are dedicating their time to write our next contract for UGAs and DDS student workers.

We’ve made strides in proposing:

  • A Bereavement clause that permits student workers to take up to three working days off with pay upon the death of a loved one.
  • Religious and disability accommodations for all the student workers we represent.
  • Housing and meal credit for UGAs.
  • Cost of Travel Reimbursement Fund—establishing a $15,000 Transportation Assistance Fund each fiscal year to cover travel to and from Dartmouth.

None of these proposals are finalized until we ratify our new contract at the end of bargaining. Unfortunately, Dartmouth is stalling on responding to key clauses. For the most up-to-date information, follow our Instagram (@swcdartmouth) and join our GroupMe!
