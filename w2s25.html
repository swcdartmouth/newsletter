Recap of 8th bargaining session

Thanks to everyone who joined our bargaining session this past Thursday!

 

We started the section by providing the college with our responses on Union Access to Facilities, Holiday Pay, DDS Dining Dollars, DDS Management Rights, and UGA Management Rights. The college then gave us their counter proposals on UGA Union Orientation, UGA training, DDS Hours of Workload and Security, and Par. E Union Security for both DDS student workers and UGAs.

Concerns Regarding Spring Term UGA Training Session

The email linked below highlights the concerns members of our UGA unit raised regarding the most recent training on Sunday, March 30th. After the event, workers expressed how responses to ICE and grief were approached during the sessions. More specifically, the session on “Know Your Rights” & “The International Student Experience Office” was reported to have been unclear and inappropriate, with UGAs being told they could lose their job if they intervened in an ICE interaction without further context or clarity, Assistant Directors being dismissive, and more.


UGAs present in the training have also reported that the session on “Understanding Grief & Peer Support” was potentially re-traumatizing and exhausting. SWCD crafted this email to be sent to the Office of Residential Life and we will update you with their response.
